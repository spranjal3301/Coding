! Pub/Sub (publish-subscribe) and Message Queues
*   Pub/Sub is a messaging pattern where publishers publishes/send events/messages to pub-sub(environment) and
*   subscribers subscribe/receive events/messages that they are interested in.

! Message Queues
*   Message Queues are a way of sending messages between applications, systems, or services.
*   It is a form of asynchronous service-to-service communication .
*   Messages are stored on the queue until they are processed and deleted.
*   Message Queues are often used when backend systems too busy/slow to process requests/task immediately.
